# mapswipe-project
### Putting the World's Most Vulnerable Communities on the Map

## Our Mission
We use MapSwipe to help organizations like Doctors Without Borders (MSF) identify unmapped communities. Our goal is to ensure that no one is left behind during disaster relief or medical emergencies.

## How It Works
1. **Analyze:** We use satellite imagery to identify human settlements.
2. **Validate:** Our team cross-checks data to ensure accuracy.
3. **Map:** The data is pushed to OpenStreetMap for use by real-world medics and rescuers.
